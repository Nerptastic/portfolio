About Me

My name is Nicholas Paolucci

I am a Graphic designer focused on front-end web development but I do all types of stuff. I strive to create functional design that communicates effectively and efficiently. I do a bit of everything (design, development, 3D, branding, packaging, direction, UI/UX, teaching, living) I'm interested in technology, art, and fusing the two to create exciting new things.

I currently work as an Art Director, however feel free to contact me if you are interested in working together.
